Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions I receive from potential clients.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website typically takes 1-2 weeks from contact to finish, while complex web applications can take up to a month. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Do you work with clients outside of the Midwest?

Absolutely! While I'm based in Iowa, I work with clients nationwide. Most of my communication happens via email, phone, and video calls, making location irrelevant.

What's included in your maintenance service and are you required to maintain my website?

My maintenance service includes regular updates, security monitoring, backups, bug fixes, and minor content updates. I also provide monthly performance reports and priority support. But this is all totally optional, and if your website doesn't need maintained, I can just hand it over. We can arrange some way where you could make updates yourself.

Can you help with SEO and digital marketing?

Yes! I build all websites with SEO best practices and can help with basic digital marketing strategies. For advanced marketing needs, I work with trusted partners.

What if I need changes after the project is complete?

I provide 30 days of free minor adjustments after launch. For larger changes or new features, I offer low rates and priority scheduling for existing clients. Maintenance rates start at $50 dollars a month and will only exceed $100 in special cases

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

I can help you choose and set up hosting and domains, but I recommend you own these directly. This gives you full control and avoids vendor lock-in.
